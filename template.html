Bavaria Yachts USA set to debut its first Motor Yacht to hit the
West Coast of the United States.
This week, at the Newport Beach Boat Show, Bavaria Yachts will be debuting the Virtess 420 Coupe.  This yacht is perfectly suited for the West Coast boater with an enclosed salon and three stateroom/two head layout.  The aft cockpit has moveable furniture, allowing the owner to modify the layout depending on use.  Why should the cockpit have the same layout when at the dock as when underway?  This innovative feature is something completely new to the market and is a key selling feature designed by Bavaria Yachts.  In addition to having an innovative cockpit, there is plenty of room to enjoy the weather on a nice day with a forward sunpad on the bow, as well as a large sunpad on the salon roof, usually offered only in larger vessels over 60 feet.  There is a lot of boat packed into this 42 foot package.  For the west coast yachtsman who is seeking something different, this German built yacht is worth a look.   
Bavaria will be on D Dock during the event held from May 14th to May 17th at Lido Marina Village.  After the event, the 420 Virtess Coupe will be moved to Bavaria’s factory-direct sales office at the Kona Kai Resort & Marina in San Diego.
