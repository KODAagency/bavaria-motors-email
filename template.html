Annual Customer Appreciation Days
The Bavaria Yachts Factory hosted its annual Customer Appreciation Days event this past weekend at the factory in Giebelstadt, Germany.  During the two day event, over 2,000 visitors from all over the world experienced the legendary state-of-the-art Bavaria production facility with a plant tour as well as the opportunity to see every model on display.  As with any event in Germany, there were plenty of beverages and food on hand, as well as an entertainment area for children. Fun was had by all.  We look forward to next year’s event!
