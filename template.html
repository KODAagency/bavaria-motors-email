Tom Debacco and Kate Payne are documenting their adventure
on their blog.



We invite you to follow along as they live an adventure most only dream of. Check in now and then at www.firstmatekate.com to see what they are up to. And be sure to click on “Track Our Trip” (at the top of every blog post) for great stories, images, and real-life cruising details.
